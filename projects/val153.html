VAL153 Unity 6 Master project

An indie-style espionage game developed in Unity 6 during my Master’s degree. Play as an undercover agent in a luxury hotel, where every character hides a secret — and a single suitcase can change everything.

Concept & Project Vision

Team & Roles

VAL-153 is a narrative stealth game developed in Unity 6 as part of our Master’s program. We are a team of 15 people composed of Game Designers, Developers, 2D & 3D Artists, Sound Designers, and UI/UX Artists. My role in the project is Lead Developer and Technical Level Designer.

Workflow & Organisation

I manage the project repository through Git using a clear workflow based on feature branches, merge requests, and regular integration into the main branch. This ensures clean version control and prevents conflicts between developers.

Core Gameplay & Level Design

I developed the entire core gameplay system, including player movement, camera logic, and suitcase interaction mechanics. I also designed and built the prototype level map directly in Unity, focusing on layout, navigation, and gameplay readability.

Build & Delivery

I created the first playable build of the project, ensuring stable compilation, controller support, and performance consistency across devices. This build serves as the official base version for the team’s ongoing development.
